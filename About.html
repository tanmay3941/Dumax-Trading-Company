Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

How much paint do I need for my room? +

One liter of Dumax paint typically covers 120-140 square feet with one coat. For a standard 10x12 feet room with 10 feet ceiling, you would need approximately 3-4 liters for two coats. Use our online calculator or contact our support team for precise estimates.

What is the warranty on Dumax paints? +

Our Interior Premium Emulsion comes with a 5-year warranty, while our Exterior Weather Shield is backed by a comprehensive 10-year warranty against peeling, flaking, and fading when applied as per our guidelines.

Are Dumax paints eco-friendly and safe? +

Yes! All Dumax paints are low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) and meet international environmental standards. They are safe for children's rooms and have minimal odor, making them ideal for occupied spaces.

How long does it take for the paint to dry? +

Dumax paints are touch-dry within 30-45 minutes and ready for a second coat in 4-6 hours. Full curing takes 7-10 days. Drying time may vary based on weather conditions, humidity, and ventilation.

Can I get a custom color mixed? +

Absolutely! We offer custom color matching services at all our authorized dealers. Bring in a color sample, fabric swatch, or even a photo, and our advanced tinting machines can create your perfect shade from over 2,300 colors.

Do you provide professional painting services? +

Yes, we have a network of certified professional painters across India. Contact us for a free quote, and we'll connect you with experienced painters in your area who are trained in Dumax application techniques.
